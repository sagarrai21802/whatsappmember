WhatsApp Group Members Scraper

Version 1.0.0

A Chrome extension to scrape WhatsApp group members and export them to CSV format. Simply open a group, view all members, and the extension automatically collects phone numbers, names, and descriptions.

Features

  • Auto-injects on WhatsApp Web
  • Extracts phone numbers, names, and descriptions
  • Persistent storage - data survives browser restart
  • Export to CSV with one click
  • Tracks which group members came from
  • Draggable floating UI
